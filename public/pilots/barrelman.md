Born on Io, Aleksandra had her whole life changed by the corporation known as SSC (Smith-Shimano Corpro), a corporation dedicated to posthumanist research developing advanced cybernetic and organic implants. Her and the remaining townspeople were offered a choice, to volunteer themselves willingly or die, many choosing the latter. After her town militia was crushed by SSC death squads, her and the remaining survivors were taken to a black site on Europa, where their bodies submitted for testing. Tests included trialling of cybernetics, and the harvesting of biomass and organics from their own bodies. As a result, most of Aleksandra's body is cybernetic, including her internals, eyes, the lower half of her face, and everything below the neck. She spent most of her youth in this black site, until the age of 21, where prisoners who were conscripted to become guards of the facility staged a mutiny. She joined the breakout, and escaped in an SSC cargo ship, containing multiple mechs and technologies. The escaped group established themselves as the 'RED MUTINEERS' , a pirate faction who operated around the SSC's supply lines on Saturn. At the age of 24, she joined HELIOS, a mercenary company currently based on Peregrine Orbital Station.
