A defect facsimile clone that walked out of the factory. Spent time as a civilian, working and getting fired from various menial jobs, until a recruiter found him, where a natural aptitude for mech piloting was discovered. He tells people his name is "John Smith". It is most certainly one he came up with on the spot. He earnt the callsign "Nine" because his head's always in the clouds. Clouds, cloud nine, nine. Nine spaces out constantly and often has trouble paying attention to briefings.

## Trivia
- Nine has an accent that indicates he comes from the deep south of America. He can recite almost any Bible line if prompted.

- Besides mechanised chassis, Nine is proficient in piloting farming and logging equipment.

- Nine has a cat. Her name is Max.

- Nine’s hobbies are petting his cat, listening to music, keeping his knowledge of soldiering and warfare up to date, maintaining his belongings, and reading the Bible. He is not religious.

- Nine’s preferred sort of music is best described as “quiet”, “dreamlike”, or “dissociative”.

- Nine does not have a favourite food. He eats what he is given, within reason. He does seem to like apple juice.

- The only personal customisation Nine makes to his mechs is the installation of a sound system within the cockpit. Otherwise, they are completely factory standard and built for military efficiency.

- Nine does not know what sex looks like. He is not interested in knowing.

- Nine is willing to die for you, upon reasonable request.

- Outside of his mech, Nine is a proficient rifleman. He scored a 17 out of 20 on his last marksmanship examination.

- Nine is a good source of information on the most cost-efficient sources of sustenance and how to produce them, best workouts to target any muscle group within the human body, and best practices in weapon maintenance.

- Nine is chronologically five years old. He does not know how old he is biologically.
