# Mission // #002
## STRIKE & SEIZE
### MISSION BRIEF
**OBJECTIVE PRIORITY: HIGH**
**TARGET CLASSIFICATION: POLITICAL / STRATEGIC**

**TARGET**: Rafiq Karam – Confirmed member of the **OPEC COUNCIL.** Recent intel indicates Karam is preparing to flee the region. Satellite intercepts and HUMINT place him en route to Riyadh International Airport via the Eastern Ring Road, utilizing a secure VIP escape corridor.

### PRIMARY GOAL:

Intercept and capture or eliminate Rafiq Karam

Engagement is authorized only after positive ID confirmation

Civilian collateral must be minimized – area includes high-density transit corridors and airport zones

### ENGAGEMENT POINTS:

Preferred: Intercept at Eastern Ring Road checkpoint (less civilian interference, easier exfil)

Fallback: Airstrip sector near private tarmac terminals – higher civilian density, restricted airspace risk

### MISSION STAKES:

Failure to secure or neutralize Rafiq will prolong conflict and reduce political pressure on **OPEC**, stalling contract completion

If captured alive: leverage value as a strategic hostage

If confirmed **KIA**: escalates pressure; sends decisive message to remaining council members

### COMMAND NOTES:

Operatives must be equipped for rapid mobility, ID verification, and non-lethal takedown options if feasible

Avoid unnecessary disruption of Riyadh civilian infrastructure; *Rules of Engagement Directive 7-C* remains in effect

Use of civilian disguise recognition systems is recommended for rapid target isolation

### AUTHORIZATION:
**IMMORTAL** will oversee operation remotely; all operational updates to be routed through PROTOCOL DUSKHOUND
